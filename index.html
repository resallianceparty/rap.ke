Message from the Party Leader and Founding Members

Welcome to the official platform of the Restoration Alliance Party (RAP). Founded in 2023 after a journey that began in 2022, RAP is a people-centered political movement grounded in the principles of Sustainable Centrism—an ideology that balances economic growth, social justice, and environmental stewardship. Our vision is to restore dignity in governance and transform Kenya into a Nation of Opportunities for all.

Our party symbol, the Spectacles, represents clarity of vision and focus on the future. With our rallying slogan “Mambo Sawa Sawa”, we embrace optimism, unity, and progressive leadership. We are guided by Five Transformative Pillars and 44 Focus Areas designed to address the core challenges facing our nation and offer practical, inclusive solutions.

RAP is not just about contesting for power—it is about building a lasting legacy of ethical leadership, institutional reform, and economic empowerment. We invite all Kenyans, regardless of background, to walk with us on this journey of national renewal. Together, we can fulfill the promise of our Constitution, honor the sacrifices of our forefathers, and realize the Kenya we all believe in—a prosperous, just, and united nation.

Let’s Restore and Progressively Transform Kenya into the Nation of Opportunities!

Mambo Sawa Sawa. A Nation of Opportunities.
