Accessibility

Resign or Check Your Membership Status

If you need to resign from RAP or verify your political party membership status, follow the simple USSD steps below:

Check Status / Resign via USSD

  1. Dial *509# on your phone.
  2. Select Review option.
  3. Enter your First Name.
  4. Enter your ID Number.
  5. You will receive a 4-digit PIN.
  6. Dial *509# again and enter the PIN.
  7. Select your Security Word and confirm.
  8. Log in again with *509# and PIN.
  9. Select Membership to confirm your status.
  10. If you wish to resign, select Resign option.

Manual Resignation

You can resign manually by downloading and completing the official resignation form:

Download FORM

Once completed, email it to the attention of the RAP Secretary General.

Manual Registration

You can also register through a RAP Recruitment Agent in your area. Check the sample manual registration form under the Resources section.
