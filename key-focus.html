Key focus areas

RAP’s Five Pillars of Restoration and Progressive Transformation, each supported by 44 carefully crafted Focus Areas.

# Pillar Focus Areas
1 Creating Opportunities and Restoring Hope
  • Building Employment Opportunities
  • Education Access and Quality for a Strong Nation
  • Affordable and Social Housing & Human Settlement – Haki ya Kuwa Na Nyumba
  • Universal Healthcare and Comprehensive Social Security – TaifaCare
  • School Feeding Programs – Empowering Young Minds
  • Arts, Culture, and Creative Industries – Celebrating Kenya’s Heritage
  • Implementing Mandatory National Youth Service – Madaraka Youth Brigade
  • Establishment of Digital Education Annuity Fund
  • Community Volunteer Programs
2 Deepening Economic Growth With Agility
  • Strengthening MSMEs and Cooperatives For Business Growth
  • Agricultural Innovation – Kilimo Biashara
  • Renewable Energy Development and Reducing Power Costs
  • Transportation Infrastructure Development
  • Sustainable Tourism Sector Development
  • Industrialisation, Trade and Blue Economy Expansion – Cottage Industries & Industrial Parks
  • Digital Economy Growth – Uchumi Kidijitali
  • Supporting County-Government-Led Development – Pesa Mashinani
  • Innovation in Storage Facilities and Logistics – Enhancing Cold Chain and Trade Infrastructure
  • Accelerating Money Circulation - The gig economy
3 Strengthening the Financial Sector Capacity
  • Access to Affordable Credit
  • Creating a Special Administrative Region for Financial and Trade Growth – Financial Hub
  • Empowering Economic Diplomacy and Diaspora Affairs
  • Advancing Financial Innovation
  • Enhancing Public-Private Partnerships (PPPs) and Foreign Direct Investment (FDI) For Growth
  • Establishing an Integrated Development Bank for Key Economic Sectors and Impact Investing
  • Comprehensive Banking Sector Reforms
  • Pension System Reforms and Insurance Access Expansion
  • Effective Foreign Trade Policies for Mutual Benefit
4 Pioneering People’s Resilience and Unity
  • Open National Dialogue & Ethnic Cohesion
  • Promoting Special Interest Groups Participation in Governance and Economic Activities
  • Youth and Sports Development
  • Disaster Preparedness
  • Social Protection Programs and Mental Health Wellbeing
  • Inclusive Water & Sanitation Infrastructure Programs – Maji Milele
  • Intergenerational Reconciliation, Land Justice, and Community Mediation Programs
  • Supporting PWDs for Self-Reliance – Disability Has Its Own Unique Abilities
  • Climate Adaptation and Resilience – Tumalize Ukame Kenya
5 Creating a Culture of Stewardship Governance
  • Anti-Corruption Measures – Tukomeza Ufisadi, Imarisha Uadilifu
  • Electoral Integrity – Imarisha Uchaguzi
  • Rule of Law and Security Reforms
  • Enhancing Governance and Public Institution Services – Huduma Bora
  • Equitable Tax Policies – Kodi Nafuu
  • Strengthening Economic Resilience through Macroeconomic and Monetary Policy Reform
  • Prudent Public Debt Management
