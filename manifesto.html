Our Manifesto

The RAP Manifesto is structured around Five Pillars of Restoration and Progressive Transformation which guide our plans for Kenya’s future.

1

Creating Opportunities & Restoring Hope

RAP champions sustainable, people-driven development by promoting job creation, industrialization, and agricultural modernization. We prioritize inclusive economic policies that ensure every Kenyan has the opportunity to achieve economic success, self-sufficiency, and a fulfilling life.

2

Deepening Economic Growth with Agility

RAP is committed to building a robust, diversified economy by fostering an environment that supports private sector growth, local enterprise, and foreign investment. Through strategic investment in ICT and innovation, we aim to make Kenya globally competitive, encouraging technological advancements that drive economic transformation and modern public services.

3

Strengthening Financial Sector Capacity

RAP envisions Kenya as Africa’s premier financial and trade hub. By enhancing financial systems, RAP aims to attract foreign direct investment to accelerate economic recovery and sustainable growth, positioning Kenya as a leader in liberal financial markets within Africa.

4

Pioneering Resilience and Unity

RAP values Kenya’s cultural, ethnic, and regional diversity, seeing it as a cornerstone of national strength. We promote national unity through reconciliation and inclusivity, empowering youth and women, while ensuring equal representation and active participation in Kenya’s governance and leadership.

5

Stewardship in Governance

Guided by principles of dignity, integrity, and servant leadership, RAP promotes accountable, people-centered leadership that builds public trust, enhances transparency, and delivers quality services. We are committed to eradicating corruption in all its forms, enforcing strict anti-corruption measures, and upholding the rule of law to protect public resources. RAP also advocates for environmental sustainability, land reforms, and responsible management of Kenya’s natural resources, preserving our heritage for future generations.
