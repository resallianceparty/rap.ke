Our Manifesto

The RAP Manifesto is structured around Five Pillars of Restoration and Progressive Transformation which guide our plans for Kenya’s future.

1

Creating Opportunities & Restoring Hope

Focus on youth empowerment, education, healthcare, housing, and employment creation.

2

Deepening Economic Growth with Agility

Empower MSMEs, agriculture, tourism, and digital infrastructure for inclusive growth.

3

Strengthening Financial Sector Capacity

Enable county-based economies, financial inclusion, banking reforms, and investment access.

4

Pioneering Resilience and Unity

Strengthen national cohesion, climate action, social protection, and mental wellness.

5

Stewardship in Governance

Deliver transparency, justice system reforms, public service accountability, and integrity.
