Our Ideology

Sustainable Centrism is RAP’s unique political and governance ideology. It balances responsible economic growth, social inclusion, justice, and environmental care to ensure long-term national sustainability.

It is about building a political and governance culture that centers on:

  • Balanced development: Not extreme left or right policies, but strategic, inclusive decisions.
  • Opportunity for all: Creating structures and systems that serve every Kenyan regardless of origin.
  • Progressive unity: Encouraging togetherness across all diversities—ethnic, social, generational.

Why Sustainable Centrism?

Because Kenya’s current governance model has polarized and marginalized communities. Sustainable Centrism bridges the gap by being:

  • Issue-based rather than tribal or personality-based
  • Action-oriented rather than promise-heavy
  • Future-focused rather than reactive
