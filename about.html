About RAP

The Restoration Alliance Party (RAP) is a political movement rooted in the ideology of Sustainable Centrism. It was established in 2023 with a mission to restore hope and usher in a transformative era of governance in Kenya.

Vision

A Transformed Kenya – the Nation of Opportunities

Mission

To restore hope and deliver people-centered governance that is transformative, equitable, inclusive and sustainable.

Founding

  • Founded: February 2023
  • Party Symbol: Spectacles
  • Party Slogan: “Mambo Sawa Sawa”

Core Values

  • Faith
  • Justice
  • Hard Work
  • Hope
  • Integrity
  • Stewardship
  • Resilience

Party Organs

RAP’s structure includes the following key organs:

  • National Delegates Congress
  • National Governing Council
  • National Executive Committee
  • National Secretariat
  • County Congress
  • Sub-County/Constituency Branch
  • Ward Branch
  • Party Caucuses (Women, Youth, Diaspora, Disability, etc.)
