Our Slogan

The party slogan “Mambo Sawa Sawa” (Things are already better) embodies RAP’s ethical and moral vision of a Kenya where opportunity is a universal right rather than a privilege reserved for a select few under RAP’s Manifesto flagship banner “The Nation of Opportunities”. This vision rests on three core ethical principles:

    1.Dignity and Respect for All: RAP acknowledges that every Kenyan, regardless of their background, deserves respect and the right to live a dignified life. Policies will ensure access to resources and opportunities that empower individuals to achieve their potential.
    2.Accountability and Integrity: Guided by the value of servant leadership, RAP emphasizes governance practices that prioritize the common good over personal or political gain. Leaders are expected to act transparently and ethically in managing Kenya’s resources and affairs.
    3.Stewardship of Resources: RAP recognizes the responsibility to manage Kenya’s natural and economic resources wisely, ensuring their preservation for future generations while creating wealth and opportunities for the present.
